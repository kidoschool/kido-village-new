Kido village is a platform for parents to find teachers and caregivers in their neighbourhood for their child’s preschool and daycare needs It is also a platform for Teachers/ aspiring teachers and women looking to start their own learning station. The platform is set up to deliver quality, affordable, early education from your house or a parents home.

You become part of the Global Kido Group. You deliver a world class curriculum from the comfort of your own home. You have complete control of your schedule. So you choose the hours you work.

As a registered teacher you may conduct multiple batches of no more than 8 children per batch. You may earn upto Rs. 50000 a month based on the number of children and batches you decide to do.

All advertising is done by us and hence only interested parents will reach out to you. You as a teacher don't have to market your skillset. The platform showcases you and allows parents to touch base with you directly.
